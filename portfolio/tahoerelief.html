Lake Tahoe Mountain Bike Adventure

I live in Tahoe and love to Mountain Bike. I wanted to make a terrain map to highlight a way to mountain bike around the entire lake. The layers and hillshade were generated using ArcPro, the shaded relief was edited in Photoshop, and the final graphic was produced using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.
