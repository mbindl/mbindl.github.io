A Forest at Risk - Lake Tahoe's West Shore

I was tasked with creating a compelling infographic to tell a visual story. I had to think through how to distill information into a clear, informative story that educated the reader on the current condition of Lake Tahoe's west shore forests, explaining why overstocked forests are an issue, where the issue is exacerbated, and why it's important to do something about it.
