A Forest at Risk - Lake Tahoe's West Shore

I was tasked with creating an infographic to tell a visual story. I developed a clear and informative story that educated the reader on the current condition of Lake Tahoe's west shore forests, explaining why overstocked forests are an issue, which parameters exacerbate it, and why it's important to do something about it.
