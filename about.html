About

TL;DR: Behind the computer, I'm a GIS Analyst with a developer focus. I’m drawn to technology's capacity to disrupt and create change, especially towards humanitarian and environmental innovation.

I currently work for the Tahoe Regional Planning Agency building digital mapping applications and data services. I'm also pursuing an M.S. in Cartography, GIS, and Web Map Programming from the University of Wisconsin-Madison.

Away from the computer, I enjoy spending time outdoors (skiing, hiking, paddle boarding, mountain biking), traveling, and exploring new places.

I'm a native Wisconsinite, raised in Spring Green, WI and currently based in South Lake Tahoe, CA.

Background: I discovered "GIS" during my sophmore year at the University of Wisconsin - Milwuakee while visiting American Geographical Society Library. I was immediately impressed by its effectiveness as a communication medium and intrigued by its amalgamation of data and design. I secured a position filing maps away, reshelving old National Geogrpahics, and cataloging geospatial data. From there I was hooked.
